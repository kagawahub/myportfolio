About Bossman

Background

I’m Bossman, a web developer from Ghana. I am passionate about developing beautiful websites for customers all around the world. A student of Accra Technical University and an intern at GIT Plus Ghana. Web Dev has been a wonderful course of life. I’m always opened to learning new things and sharing of ideas as I continue to share in developing many wonderful projects.

What I do

I build unique sites to fit the needs of corporate institutions and individuals all over the world. This is done from scratch with the use of HTML, CSS and Javascript. Thus, a fully customised project is achieved. Another option is with the use of WordPress which I admire a lot. As a front-end developer, I take care of the website appearance to users. I make sure websites look as beautiful as they should be. I also ensure that the web pages are user-friendly where users can interact with the site's interface with ease.
